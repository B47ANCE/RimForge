# Code of Conduct

RimForge contributors and users are expected to communicate respectfully, assume good faith, and focus criticism on ideas and code rather than people.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, and sustained disruptive behavior are not acceptable in project spaces. Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when necessary to protect the community.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through GitHub. Reports will be reviewed as promptly and fairly as possible.
